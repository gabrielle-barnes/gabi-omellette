Ballona Wetlands - Camera Trap Workflow

While a volunteer, I used AddaxAI, MegaDetector and SpeciesNet to analyze the camera trap photos they had (animals & people are labeled). Got the idea to integrate the technology from the work of Dan Morris!

MSR Undergrad - LLM-Augmented Genetic Algorithm

I did an internship @ Microsoft Research as an undergraduate and worked on integrating LLMs into a genetic algorithm to improve the capabilities of finding molecules with specific desired properties. My direct mentor was Bichlien Nguyen and I worked closely with the whole Agora team!

Synthergy - AI Social Deception Game

For my undergrad senior group project, I designed and improved the AI agents' behavior for a social-deception game called Synthergy. We were advised by Prof. Andrew Forney and Aidan Srouji!

Dr. Toal's PLE Research

As an undergrad I worked with Dr. Ray Toal on his research related to programming languages by coding in and documenting 100+ languages. It was my first insight into research!
